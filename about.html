Who is Mikaye


Mikaye Hair and Beauty, was founded in 2018, specializes in catering to the unique hair care needs of African women. They offer loc solutions, challenging conventional beauty standards by celebrating diverse African hair textures. Mikaye provides tailored products and high-quality services to empower and boost the confidence of women while ensuring their satisfaction. Ultimately, the company envisions a future where African women feel like queens, fully embracing their unique beauty and taking control of their hair journeys.
